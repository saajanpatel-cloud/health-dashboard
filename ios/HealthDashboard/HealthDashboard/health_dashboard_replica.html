Maintenance (kcal/day)
Prefill from logs averages dietary kcal from your Apple Health export (same field as the dashboard CSV). Window: latest 14 calendar days (needs 5 days with kcal logged), else latest 28 days (needs 7). The header status line lists the date range used.
Macro presets

Muscle build — P / C / F ≈ 30 / 45 / 25 % of kcal. Pins nutrition total to maintenance + 250 kcal (if maintenance is empty: current macro total + 250), then sets grams from those %.

Fat loss — P / C / F ≈ 35 / 40 / 25 % of kcal. Pins nutrition total to maintenance − 250 kcal (if maintenance is empty: current macro total − 250), then sets grams from those %.
